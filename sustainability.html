Sustainability at Deccan

As a fast-growing custom manufacturing business, Deccan is dedicated to lowering our environmental impacts and implementing sustainable practices. We have implemented an ESG strategy with the theme “Embracing Sustainability” comprising 4 pillars and 15 focus areas that are important to our business. In accordance with this framework, we have also established goals for a three to five year horizon.

Chemicals manufacturing is an inherently resource intensive process. Our extensive focus on ESG performance helps substantially reduce social and environmental impacts. This also goes a long way in supporting our valued customers world-wide who are keen on keeping their value chain impacts to a minimum.
